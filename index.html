Hi, I'm Jeeyoung Lee.

I'm Jeeyoung Lee, a PhD candidate in the Department of Government at Harvard University.

I study comparative political economy of advanced democracies. My research focuses on the politics of housing and urban development in the context of economic restructuring and the rise of the knowledge economy.

Before starting my PhD at Harvard, I received a BA in Political Science and Economics and an MA in Political Science from Korea University. I grew up in Seoul, South Korea, and my research is inspired by my home city.
